Logistics coordinators at H.Essers spent significant time context-switching between Outlook and internal dispatch systems. Shipment data arriving by email had to be manually re-entered

02 / APPROACH

I designed and built an Outlook Add-in that lets users submit selected emails and their attachments to a downstream system. 03 / SOLUTION

Coordinators can forward an email directly from Outlook without leaving the app. The backend processes it asynchronously, stores the content, and triggers the downstream workflow — fully logged and traceable end to end
